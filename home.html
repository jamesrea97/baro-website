About baro


baro is a charitable organization devoted to making the world a better place through empowering communities to learn using our peer to peer learning app. Together, we can make a difference in the world for generations to come. Whether you want to contribute with money or time, read more about our work and learn how you can make a change today.



Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is for knowledge not to be a commodity. We are looking to create a platform where users in the developing world can create teaching content in their native tongue and share it with their community. With or without the need for WiFi.


VISION: Our vision is that with the use of technology we can help share knowledge to those in the most remote places. Smartphones in Africa have had a boom of adoption in the past few years with over 77% of Africans under 35 owning a smartphone. However less than 40% of people in Africa have reliable access to the internet. With our app we can put the technology they already have to good use, by using this to bring them learning materials.

About the App


What we have created

At baro our mission is to create a peer to peer app for people in low internet environments to create and share learning materials between themselves. We have achived this by making an android app that allows users to create materials and share over bluetooth.

A simple to navigate home screen.

Share Screen

This is where a user can either choose to upload their courses to the internet or share over bluetooth.
